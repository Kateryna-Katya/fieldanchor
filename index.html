Anchor

Drop Anchor. Hold Your Ground.

It's basically a game about staying still - sounds
boring, but trust me, it's not.

Features

  • Stay the Course

    Every level tests your ability to resist — tap at the wrong moment and you drift.

  • Clean, Focused Design

    No clutter, no noise. Just you, the anchor, and the pull of the current.

  • One-Tap Mastery

    Easy to learn but man, it'll humble you real quick. You think you've got it figured out, then boom - you're starting over.

  • Endless Replayability

    Every time you play, it throws something different at you, so you can't just memorize patterns.

How It Works

  • Drop In

    Launch a run and feel the tension build from the very first second.

  • Hold or Release

    You've gotta time when to drop your anchor just right. Rush it and you're done for.

  • Survive Longer

    Each run pushes further than the last, rewarding focus and steady nerves.
